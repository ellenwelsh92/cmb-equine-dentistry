About Charlotte Bishop

Charlotte Bishop studied Equine Dental Science at Hartpury College, Gloucester. On completion of the three year degree course, Charlotte was awarded a 2:1 BSc (Hons).


Charlotte then spent a further 18 months as an apprentice to Chris Keate in order to enhance her practical skills and futher her knowledge within the specialized field of equine dentistry.


Since graduating Charlotte has also been awarded the BEVA/BVDA qualification in equine dentistry.


Charlotte has built CMB Equine Dentistry, and is proud and passionate about providing a calm,professional and honest approach when carrying out routine dental treatments.
